Our Mission

Most typing platforms only test in English. We built 804 Typing because keyboard fluency matters in every language — whether you write in Arabic, Urdu, Chinese, or Portuguese. Our goal is to make professional-grade typing practice accessible to every person on earth, in their own language.

What We Offer

  • Real grammatical sentences — not random word lists — in 12+ languages
  • Time-based mode from 15 seconds up to 10 minutes, and word-count mode
  • Three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard sentence sets
  • Phonetic on-screen keyboards for every language, including an LTR phonetic mode for Urdu & Arabic that lets you type native script using ordinary English keys
  • Five kid-friendly arcade games — Word Rush, Balloon Pop, Bug Squash, Letter Rain, and Rocket Race — to make daily practice fun
  • Typing sound effects for satisfying keypress feedback (toggle on/off anytime)
  • Optional accounts to track WPM, accuracy, and typing history over time
  • Progressive Web App — installable and works fully offline once loaded
